Types of Narrative Text 📚

1. Fable

A fable is a story that uses animals as characters and teaches a moral lesson. The animals usually act like humans.

Example: The Lion and the Mouse

2. Myth

A myth is a traditional story that explains natural events or beliefs, often involving gods or supernatural beings.

Example: Stories about gods and creation of the world

3. Legend

A legend is a story about a historical event or a place, which may not be completely true but is believed by people.

Example: The Legend of Surabaya

4. Fairy Tale

A fairy tale is a story that contains magic, fantasy, and imaginary characters such as fairies, princes, or witches.

Example: Cinderella

5. Science Fiction

Science fiction is a story about future life, technology, or space.

Example: Stories about robots or space travel

6. Horror

Horror is a story that is made to scare the reader. It often contains ghosts or frightening events.

Example: Ghost stories

7. Mystery

A mystery is a story that involves a problem or crime that must be solved.

Example: Detective stories

8. Historical Story

A historical story is a narrative based on real events in the past, although it may include imagination.

Example: Stories about heroes or wars
